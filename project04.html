Where it all started

“one of the CRAZIEST pieces of artwork made for me, the details man omg.. much love bro”

If it wasn't for this collection of drawings, you wouldn't be browsing this webpage right now. In 2021 MIIZT let me play with him while he was streaming, and I appreciated his gesture so much, that I wanted to make something for him. Luckily enough my brother had a drawing tablet he never actually used, so I borrowed it from him to draw this picture that I already had in my mind. I quit drawing in the early 2010s, thus the first drawing you see on this page was a very difficult challenge for me. Anyway, I finished it, and sent it to Nathan.

Needless to say it was a successful piece, because I didn't plan this whole thing at all. I just wanted to make a gift for MIIZT and that's it — no art career. Well, shortly after this people started reaching out to me for commissions and this series went on for months. Along the way I realized my art skills were lacking a lot, therefore I started investing into this drawing thing more and more to the point where I finally rekindled my love for art. Through this collection you can see the progress I made in a year.

I'd like to thank everyone featured on this page! I got to know many great people, and made many friends through this.
